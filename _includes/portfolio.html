About Me

Greetings! I am a Masters candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University, USA. For the past 2 years, I invested considerable time solving some of India’s most challenging healthcare problems at Practo, Asia's largest health tech startup. As a software developer, I was working on creating India’s first online consultation marketplace, Practo Consult and solved critical problems such as classifying and assigning health queries to doctors to match demand-supply gaps, providing relevant data using information retrieval techniques like Elasticsearch and creating seamless chat experiences using Firebase. I dream to employ my technical knowledge in the betterment of our society and all my academic and professional endeavors in the past have been in support of realising that dream.
